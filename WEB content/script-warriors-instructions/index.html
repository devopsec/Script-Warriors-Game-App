How to Play

    Script Warriors is compatible with many Operating Systems, including Windows, Macs, and will soon be ported to Linux machines as well. Download the version for your system below, then go back to the home webpage and click Play.

    This is a text-based RPG multiplayer-online game. You will have to type commands in the console, based on what you want your character to do.
    The objective of this game is to capture the opposing teamâ€™s flag and return it to your base or eliminate each opposing team member by depleting all of their Health Points.

    Quick commands: d (weapon) - drop current weapon, p (item/weapon) - pick up item/weapon.

    TERMS:
    Stats - info HP - your current health (hit points) AP - Action points, used to move across rooms, pickup power-ups & weapons, and attack other players. Ammo - Amount of ammunition in current ranged weapon.
    POWER-UPs:
    Teleporter - allows you to move to any location on the map (uses 3 AP) Grenade - deals 25% damage to all enemies in current room & in a room in each direction (3 AP) Extended Map - increase Ammo capacity by 50%. AP Refill - refills AP to MAX. Reload - reload your current weapon to MAX. Upgrade HP - increase HP Capacity by 50% (3 AP). HP Recovery - recovers HP by 25%. Advanced Scope - increases accuracy of weapon by 15% (3 AP).
